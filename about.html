About me:

When I started this career change in year 2021, I had very little expectation of what the outcome will be. All my career years were spent working as a clerical support and I had no knowledge of any programming language whatsoever.

Now, fast forwarding to the last day of the 9-week Web Development Bootcamp of Juno College of Technology, I am still surprise that I was able to do 5 projects, which are showcased below, in a span of 9 weeks.

What amazes me more is I enjoyed every step of the development process from problem solving to seeing your very own website being live. I am very grateful and thrilled to start this new career journey and to continuously expand my skills and knowledge of Web Development.

Movie finder

html, sass, javascript, api | 2022

About the project:

This was a paired programming project and we had to create a web application using JavaScipt and a third-party API. My project partner and I created a movie finder app that generates movie results based on the user's input. We used a second endpoint for our error handling and we embedded an iframe to get the trailer to appear on the page. App is responsive to all screen sizes and is accessible for screen readers.
